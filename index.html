Frequently Answered Queries

How does booking verification work? ▼

Once you select your rental machinery dates and place an order request, the equipment host confirms availability within 24 hours. Valid regional ID paperwork is verified upon delivery.

What happens if a machine breaks down? ▼

All our active listings come equipped with standard machinery insurance. In case of mechanical failures during normal operating conditions, roadside support or field replacements are provided instantly.

Can I list my own tractor here? ▼

Yes! Use the "List Equipment" button at the top to configure your machinery details, select an hourly/daily rate tier, and open bookings up to neighboring farmers securely.
